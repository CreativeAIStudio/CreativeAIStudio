Our Family Story

Welcome to Upper Crust Pizza Cafe, where pizza dreams come true! Nestled in the heart of Celebration, FL, we are a family-owned gem serving up the finest New York-style pizzas that will make your taste buds dance with joy.

Every day, we roll out the dough, toss on the freshest ingredients, and bake our pizzas to perfection. Our menu is a delicious adventure featuring not just pizza, but also mouthwatering wings, hearty subs, crisp salads, and decadent desserts. We believe in quality, and that’s why we use only premium ingredients that would make even your grandma proud!

At Upper Crust, we don’t just serve food; we create an experience. Whether you’re grabbing a slice on the go or enjoying a meal with family and friends, our welcoming atmosphere will make you feel right at home. So come on in, grab a slice, and let’s make some memories!
